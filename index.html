I am an undergraduate student at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology . I am affiliated with the Cheng Kar-Shun Robotics Institute and the Multi-Agent Systems Laboratory, where I am fortunate to work closely with Prof. Ling Shi.

I am currently a Visiting Student Researcher at the MSC Lab at UC Berkeley, where I am advised by Prof. Masayoshi Tomizuka.

My research focuses on machine learning, learning-based control, multi-agent systems, and robotics. I am particularly interested in developing learning-based methods for unmanned robotic systems operating in complex real-world environments
